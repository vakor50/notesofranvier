About

Named after the unmylinated sheaths in the nervous system that transmit electricity throughout the body (allowing humans to feel, move, and sing), the Johns Hopkins Notes of Ranvier is Johns Hopkins' premiere service a cappella group. The Notes are dedicated to aiding the community through music, and perform at assisted living facilities, schools and hospitals--even occasionally busking in the Inner Harbor and donating all proceeds to foundations such as the Johns Hopkins Childrens Center. We also participate in Homewood Performing Arts, putting on a concert at the end of each semester, and performing for nonprofit events at the university.

We perform at Hopkins events, competitions, and upon your request.
